The Forest Operations Toolkit

10-Minute Quick Start for Learning & Development Managers

⏰ Total Setup Time: 10 Minutes

No programmers. No IT tickets. No app stores. No internet required after download.

1

Locate the Tools 1 minute

Open the folder named Zero-Three-TOOLS-READY-TO-DEPLOY. Inside you will find two files:

  • Zero-One-Medical-Patient-Concierge.html
  • Zero-Two-SOP-Training-Engine.html

✅ These are the only two files you need to deploy to stores today.

2

Test on Your Computer 2 minutes

Double-click Zero-One-Medical-Patient-Concierge.html. It will open in your web browser.

Click through the six steps. Select any options. Verify that a recommendation appears at the end with product name, staff talking points, and dosage guidance.

Close that tab. Double-click Zero-Two-SOP-Training-Engine.html. Click any scenario button. Verify that four sections appear: SOP, Compliance Check, Training Script, and Quiz.

⚠ If a file opens as text instead of a web page, right-click the file, select "Open With," and choose Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox.

3

Copy to Store Tablets 3 minutes

Choose one method:

  1. Email: Attach both HTML files to an email. Send to all store managers. Store managers download the files to their tablets.
  2. USB Drive: Copy both HTML files to a USB drive. Hand the drive to each store manager. Store manager copies files from USB to tablet.
  3. Shared Drive: Upload both HTML files to your company shared drive. Share the link with all store managers. They download to their tablets.

✅ The files work completely offline. No WiFi needed after the initial download.

4

Train Store Managers 3 minutes

Send this one-paragraph instruction to every store manager:

"Two new training tools are now on your store tablet. Open the Medical Patient Concierge when helping any medical patient or new customer. It will tell you exactly what to recommend and what to say. Open the SOP Training Engine before each shift huddle. Pick one scenario, read the training script aloud, and ask the three quiz questions. Both tools work without internet. If you have questions, contact [YOUR NAME]."
5

Verify Deployment 1 minute

Call or text one store manager. Ask them to open the Medical Patient Concierge on their tablet and walk through one scenario with you over the phone. If it works, deployment is successful at that store. Repeat for each store.

✅ You are now in production. Staff are using the tools. Customers are receiving better service.

+

What to Do Next (After Today)

These are not urgent. Do them when you have time:

  • Open Zero-Two-EDITING-GUIDES/How-to-Edit-the-Medical-Concierge.txt to add your store's actual product names and prices.
  • Open Zero-Two-EDITING-GUIDES/How-to-Edit-the-SOP-Engine.txt to add store-specific scenarios.
  • Open Zero-Four-PRINTABLES to print physical copies of the Medical Patient Playbook and 30-60-90 Day Plan.
  • Open Zero-Five-TRAINING-MODULES for pre-built weekly training schedules.
